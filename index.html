About

I received a bachelor's degree in computer science and worked as a software engineer for a couple of years before deciding that my real passion is in science that more directly impacts people. To pursue this, I am currently preparing to apply to medical school. I still enjoy coding during my free time and have a broad interest in science in general. I like to think and learn about topics (at an amateur level) ranging from behavioral economics to cosmology. I also have an appreciation for artistic things like music and design (but I'm an amateur here as well). I think because of this, the things that excite me most are at the intersection of logical problem solving and creative expression.
